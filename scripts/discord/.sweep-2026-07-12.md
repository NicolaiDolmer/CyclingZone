## TRÅD [AKTIV]: Training speed
thread: 1523716778621010141

**@thelamba** (2026-07-06 15:46 UTC):
I've already touched on it in general chat, but wanted to formally put it in here as well.

I saw that in the long run, you wanted a longer term academty -> youth team(s) -> senior team talent growth system and while I support that and think it's a really cool idea, I think there's something to say for training faster at lower skills too.

A 20 year old transitioning to cycling, who is in fact an athlete, but learning cycling techniques to improve will improve much more rapidly, than an already developed 20 year old who has been in the sport for long.

So basically my suggestion is that it should be faster to train lower skills and slower the closer you get to the potential (but still faster to reach 9 out of potential 10, than for example 35 out of 40).
