**@bobby2106** (2026-07-12 18:50 UTC):
Race engine update (v6.97)
Four changes to how races play out:
Day form - every rider now has good days and bad days. On rare days even the favorite can crack. Riders in good form crack less often.
Team work costs - helpers now lose places working for their captain, and the captain gets a much stronger boost in return. A new Free role lets a rider skip team duties and ride his own race.
Stage tactics - in stage races you can set roles and effort per stage and change tactics while the race runs. Took the jersey? One click makes that rider your captain for the rest of the race.
Crashes and mechanicals - riders can now crash or have a mechanical. Most lose time, a few abandon the race. Incidents are rare, and good field positioning reduces the risk.
Full details in the patch notes and in-game Help.
